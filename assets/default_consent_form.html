English Version
Please read the following to the Client before you access his or her clinical data.

With this phone, you can give your CHV access to information from your AMPATH medical records, including information collected on paper at AMPATH clinics such as Mosoriot Health Centre and Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital. We believe that this information may help your CHV provide services to you.

It is your decision whether or not you wish to provide your CHV with your personal health information. If you do not provide this information to your CHV, there will be no repercussions to you. You may cancel your CHV from accessing your information at any time.

Your CHV has signed a confidentiality agreement to keep your information safe and private. Your CHV is not allowed to share or discuss any of your personal health information with anyone besides you. If you would like to grant your CHV access to your information, please sign your name on the next page.

If you have any questions, or if you ever want to stop your CHV from accessing your information, you may contact your CHV, Community Health Extension Worker, or call the AMPATH phone manager directly at any time: 072-148-1324
Swahili Version
Tafadhali soma maagizo haya kwa mteja kabla ya kuchukua habari zao za kiafya.

Mhuduma wako wa Afya(CHV) akiawa na hii simu, unaweza kumpa ruhusa kutoa habari kutoka kwenye kumbukumbu yako ya matibabu kwa AMPATH AMRS, ikiwa ni pamoja na taarifa zilizokusanywa kwa karatasi katika hudhuma ya Jamii AMPATH, ama vituo kama vile Kituo cha Afya cha Mosoriot na Hospitali ya rufaa ya MTRH. Tunaamini kwamba habari hii inaweza kusaidia CHV wako kutoa huduma kwako.

Ni uamuzi wako kukubali au kukataa kutoa maelezo ya afya yako binafsi kwa CHV, hakutakuwa na madhara yeyote kwako. Unaweza kuzuia CHV wako kupata taarifa yako wakati wowote.

CHV wako ametia saini mkataba wa usiri, kuweka habari yako salama na binafsi. CHV haruhusiwi kushiriki au kujadili na yoyote kuhusu habari yako ya binafsi ya afya na mtu yeyote ila wewe. Kama ungependa kutoa ruhusa kwa CHV wako kutoa taarifa yako, tafadhali andika jina lako kwenye ukurasa unaofuata.

Kama una maswali yoyote, au kama wewe unataka kuzuia CHV wako kupata taarifa yako, unaweza kuwasiliana na CHV wako, wazimamizi wa chvs(CHEW), au piga simu kwa meneja wa simu AMPATH wakati wowote: 072-148-1324.
